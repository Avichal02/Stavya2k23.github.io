Rules

of Chitrakala

Tap on arrow to roll-back to the main page of Chitrakala

1. Students of IIITV-ICD having short attendance in academics will not be allowed to participate.

2. The Art Competition will have two prize pools respectively for online and offline modes of participation. All the students of IIITV-ICD are bound to participate only in offline mode.

3. Participants in the offline mode of participation will be given a standard A3 size sheet after the successful registration of each.

4. Participants in online mode will have to submit their entries in the size of a sheet corresponding to the same aspect ratio of the A3 sheet.

5. In case of plagiarism with regards to submission in both the modes of participation, the entry will be disqualified and organizers are not bound to notify the participant of his/her entry’s status.

6. By submitting the entry, the participant agrees that his/her artwork can be posted on Genesis Art Club’s Instagram handle (@genesis_iiitvicd).

7. Marking scheme and remark on individual entries will be shown after the completion and successful declaration of results.

8. Theme based entry is mandatory. If the entry fails to abide by the theme the entry will be disqualified

9. In case of any dispute with regard to the team and competition’s rules and regulations, the decision taken by the organizers will be final and binding.
